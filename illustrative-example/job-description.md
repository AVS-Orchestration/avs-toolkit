# Senior Product Manager - AI Solutions

## Company: InnovateCorp

## Location: Remote

## About InnovateCorp:
Oracle is a fast-growing tech company at the forefront of AI-driven solutions for enterprise clients. We leverage cutting-edge machine learning and natural language processing to build products that revolutionize workflows and create measurable business value. Our culture emphasizes innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning.

## Role Overview:
We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Senior Product Manager to lead the development and launch of our next generation of AI-powered enterprise solutions. You will own the product lifecycle from conception through launch and iteration, working closely with engineering, data science, design, and sales teams. This role requires a deep understanding of AI/ML technologies, strong product instincts, and an ability to translate complex technical concepts into clear, user-centric product requirements.

## Key Responsibilities:
*   Define and evangelize the product vision, strategy, and roadmap for AI-driven products.
*   Conduct market research, competitive analysis, and customer discovery to identify opportunities and validate product concepts.
*   Translate business needs and customer pain points into detailed product requirements (PRDs), user stories, and acceptance criteria.
*   Work closely with AI/ML engineers, data scientists, and software developers to guide product development, ensuring timely delivery and high quality.
*   Manage the product backlog, prioritize features, and make data-driven decisions to optimize product outcomes.
*   Launch new products and features, working with marketing, sales, and customer success to ensure successful go-to-market strategies.
*   Monitor product performance, analyze key metrics, and iterate on products to drive continuous improvement and achieve business goals.
*   Serve as a subject matter expert for AI/ML capabilities and applications within the organization.

## Qualifications:
*   Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Business, or a related field. Master's/MBA preferred.
*   5+ years of product management experience, with a strong focus on AI/ML or data-intensive products.
*   Proven track record of defining, launching, and iterating on successful products.
*   Solid understanding of machine learning concepts, data science workflows, and AI product development challenges.
*   Excellent communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills, with the ability to influence cross-functional teams and stakeholders.
*   Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities, with a data-driven approach to decision-making.
*   Experience with Agile methodologies and product management tools.

## Preferred Qualifications:
*   Experience in a B2B SaaS environment.
*   Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP) and MLOps practices.
*   Experience with natural language processing (NLP) applications.
